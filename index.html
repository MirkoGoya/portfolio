Asset Management System

Internal web application developed for Gruppo Intent to manage the full lifecycle of company technological assets — from assignment to return, with digital signature and complete tracking.

I contributed both as developer and project coordinator — bridging technical decisions, team communication, and product execution from day one.

The system integrates Microsoft 365 / Azure AD authentication with role-based access control across three levels. At its core, a fully autonomous workflow: every asset assignment or revocation triggers a personalized email containing a unique signing link. The employee signs directly on an HTML5 canvas — no login required — and QuestPDF automatically generates a branded PDF, delivered to both the employee and the administration.

  • Microsoft 365 / Azure AD Authentication
  • Role-based access control — SuperAdmin, Agent, User
  • Automated email pipeline with unique signing link
  • HTML5 canvas digital signature
  • Automatic PDF generation via QuestPDF
  • Excel import/export with validation
  • Dashboard with asset statistics and charts
